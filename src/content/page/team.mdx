## Here we are, Cangrejo Ideas!

Over time, we've built an extraordinary team of passionate, talented, and diverse individuals, coming from both the video game industry and other fields, united by their love for games and the desire to share that passion with everyone, even those who don't consider themselves gamers.

Within our team, you'll find artists, designers, programmers, production experts, quality control specialists, and business operations professionals, each with a unique perspective and deep mutual respect.

Cangrejo Ideas had its roots in a digital product design studio, and although we now exclusively focus on creating games, we've retained the original essence in the way we develop our titles. We always seek to challenge and reinvent industry conventions to offer unique and exciting gaming experiences.
